My Story

As what some would call an optimistic or altruistic individual, I believe technology has a lot of ways to improve the world beyond the common use case of adding convenience to everyday life. New avenues of technology have the ability to revolutionize industries through optimization and multiplied productivity. I am interested in pursuing these kinds of advancements in agriculture, transportation, finance, social services, and many other core infrastructures. With so much potential, I am always excitedly looking forward to what the future holds.

I am originially from Las Vegas, NV and moved to Seattle, WA to attend college at Seattle University. I graduated from there with a Bachelor's Degree in Computer Science with a minor in Mathematics. Shortly after graduation, I started work interning at T-Mobile working in QA doing automated testing for mobile applications on devices. I still currently work at T-Mobile almost 4 years later now a mid-level software engineer designing applications that control billions of dollars of spend and the allocation of millions of materials.

My interests in the field are web/mobile development, machine learning and AI, and microservice development. Outside of work, I enjoy spending time outdoors, playing music, cooking, video games, and quality time with friends!
